Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1.5 cups
Cocoa Powder 0.5 cup
Eggs 3

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs and milk, mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into greased pan and bake for 30-35 mins.

Extra Tips

How to make it extra moist? Add 1/4 cup of yogurt or sour cream to the batter.
Chocolate frosting idea Mix cocoa powder, powdered sugar, and butter for a quick frosting.
